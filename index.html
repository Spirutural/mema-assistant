Elder Care Voice Assistant

Assistive voice technology for seniors with visual impairments

The Elder Care Voice Assistant is a purpose-built solution designed to help visually impaired seniors maintain independence and stay connected with family. Using natural voice interaction, it provides daily support without requiring users to navigate screens or small buttons.

Features

  • Scripture & Devotionals – Audio Bible readings and daily devotional content
  • Weather & Time – Spoken weather forecasts and time announcements
  • Family Communication – Voice messages relayed to family members via SMS
  • Reminders – Medication and appointment notifications
  • Music & Hymns – Streaming gospel and traditional hymn radio

How It Works

The assistant uses a simple push-to-talk interface with large tactile buttons, making it accessible for users with limited vision. Family members receive SMS notifications when the user wants to communicate, and can call back directly.

SMS Messaging

Text messages are sent only to pre-registered family members who have explicitly opted in to receive communications. Messages originate from the elder care user and are relayed through the assistant. Recipients can opt out at any time by replying STOP.

Privacy & Consent

All SMS recipients are family members who have provided explicit consent to receive messages from this system. No marketing or promotional messages are ever sent. Message data is not shared with third parties. This service is provided solely for family communication and elder care support.
